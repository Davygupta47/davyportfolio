## Overview

Welcome to **unlearning-plm**!

This project implements parameter-efficient unlearning methods for Large Language Models (LLMs). The core goal is to selectively remove the influence of private, biased, or outdated training data from a pre-trained model without incurring the massive computational cost of retraining the model from scratch.

### Key Features
- **Multiple Unlearning Methods**: Implements gradient ascent, Ascent plus Descent (AD), and Ascent plus KL-divergence (AKL) regularization.
- **Robust Evaluation**: Features perplexity measurement pipelines to ensure general model utility remains intact after unlearning.
- **MIA Defense Validation**: Built-in Membership Inference Attack (MIA) evaluation suite utilizing ROC/AUC metrics to mathematically verify the success of data erasure.
